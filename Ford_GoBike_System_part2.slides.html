Ford GoBike System Data Analysis.¶

by : Assem Salama¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation I looked at the characteristics of all the bike sharing process that could be used to predict the rushing hours and days.Thus,I wanted to know the time in which most of the bikes was taken in terms of hours of the day and days of the week, how this was going to influence the average duration trip , and does subscribers and customers behave differently?. So,the main focus was on attributes like duration_mins , user_type , and timing information.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset provides a group of information about rides in company called Ford GoBike. The provided data shows all company's activities only around San Francisco Bay Area in month of February. The data consists of approximately 183,412 rides information.The attributes included the start time , end time , as well as many additional clients' personal information such as birth year , gender.Twelve thousand data points were removed from the analysis due to inconsistencies or missing information.

Distribution of Trip Duration.¶

The most frequent ride duration among all trips is between 5 to 10 mins. while there are two equal groups of individuals prefer to have the bike for a 5 mins or from 10 to 15 mins.

When are most trips taken in terms of time of day?¶

According to the figure below there are 2 spikes have been occuring in terms of biking use, the first one is at 8 Am and it remains steady untill it go for the second spike at 5 PM. In my opinion, these 2 spikes have strong correlation with going to work at 8 AM and back to home at 5 PM.

When are most trips taken in terms of day of the week?¶

It's way too clear that the use of biking is increasing aligned with the workdays and gets down at weekends. Additionally, Thursday is the one that gets the heighest percentage in terms of biking use with a slight differnces among the another workdays.

Average trip duration for each User Status.¶

The average trip duration for Casual customers is more than those who had a subscription by about double the period.Thus,that may bring up the idea that subscribers is using bikes for going to particular places like work within their town which takes less time.

For subscribers the most number of rides was done in Tuesday & Thursday.It seems obvious now that subscribers use the bikes the most in the going to work at 8 AM and going home at 5 PM, whereas for customer people the line is almost flat without any spikes.Thing to keep in mind that The vast majority of subscribers are men.
